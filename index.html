About

Researcher, Engineer, Builder.

I'm Shariar Kabir, a Researcher at the School of Computing, University of Portsmouth, where I work on the Horizon Europe XTRUST 6G project — developing next-generation Zero Trust Architecture systems for secure 6G networks.

I hold an MSc in Cyber Security and Forensics (Distinction) from the University of Portsmouth and a BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Daffodil International University. My research spans AI-powered security systems, deepfake detection, and building infrastructure platforms that make cybersecurity education hands-on and accessible.

I created DeepGuard — a novel AI model for detecting real vs. AI-generated media, and CyberRange.world — a full-scale virtualisation lab platform now actively used by multiple universities for daily cybersecurity lab sessions and training exercises.

DeepGuard
